What is being priced?

An equity option is a contract giving the holder the right — but not the obligation — to buy or sell shares of a stock at a fixed strike price on or before the expiration date. The engine computes the theoretical fair value of that contract using a CRR binomial tree and Quantum Amplitude Estimation.

Call Right to buy at the strike. Gains value as the stock price rises above the strike.
Put Right to sell at the strike. Gains value as the stock price falls below the strike.
